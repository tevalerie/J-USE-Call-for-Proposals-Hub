At a glance

Funding

Catalytic grant capital from Global Affairs Canada (GAC) channelled through the Environmental Foundation of Jamaica. Larger projects can apply if they secure co-financing.

Project Duration

What you'll need before you start

Prepare these so you can complete the application in one sitting (~45 min). The form auto-saves every 30 seconds and you can download a draft as JSON to resume later.

Required for All Applicants

  • Letter of Intent and Authorisation (PDF) Signed by an authorised representative within the current call period.
  • Detailed Budget Breakdown (Excel or PDF) Use the J-USE NbCS Budget Template — JMD only, max JMD $15M total.
  • Organisational Capacity / Previous Work (PDF, max 3 pages) Track record, key staff, past projects with similar scope.
  • Bank account in your organisation's legal name Hard eligibility criterion — EFJ cannot disburse without one.

Required for NGOs / Cooperatives / Faith-Based / Private

  • Organisation Registration Certificate (PDF) Companies Office, Cooperative Societies registry, etc.

Government / SOE / Informal CBO

  • Registration Certificate is not required Government & SOEs: provide Ministry/Agency Head contact instead. Informal CBOs: a community leader's letter of support.
